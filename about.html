About Me

My Story

I'm Shanti Priya, a BCA student at Amity University Jharkhand, currently in my 4th semester. I grew up in Ranchi, Jharkhand, and discovered my love for technology early — always curious about how things work behind the screen.

My frontend journey began when I first changed a button's colour with CSS and felt the instant feedback of the browser — it felt like magic. That moment ignited a passion that I've been chasing ever since. I started learning HTML and CSS, moved into JavaScript, and have recently been diving deep into React and TypeScript.

I'm a quick learner who thrives on challenges. When I took on the HENNGE Global Internship admission challenge — building a production-grade React form with API integration, error handling, and full accessibility support — I completed all three missions successfully. That experience showed me I can deliver real, polished work even as a beginner.

Beyond coding, I'm a competitive Table Tennis player (2nd prize, National Sports Day) and a painter (1st prize, Anuvrat Painting Competition, Jharkhand level). I believe creativity in art and creativity in code come from the same place.
